Deck OLED vs ROG Ally Z1E

Rog Ally's advantages compared to deck OLED:
- Z1E often delivers a 5-10% performance boost...
- Windows compatibility with many softwares such as editing softwares and Microsoft exclusives
- 1080p, 120hz screen adjustable to 720p
- Speakers have slightly more bass

Steamdeck OLED advantages compared to ROG Ally z1e:
- Runs Native SteamOS
- Track/touchpads with haptic feedback
- User friendly
- More comfortable
- Much more customisable
...etc

Disadvantages of ROG Ally z1e compared to Deck OLED:
- Windows isn't always great
- Constant blue screens and crashes
- Armoury crate updates... etc

DECK OLED Disadvantages compared to ROG ally z1e:
- Compatibility for windows-only software
- Less powerful
- Static on wired headphones... etc

Overall:
It really just depends on your choice. Steam Deck = Steam games. Ally = Roblox, Minecraft, etc. Emulation = Steam Deck!
